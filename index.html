Menu

Paneer Momo

A delightful vegetarian option filled with soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese), mixed with aromatic spices and herbs. Perfect for those looking for a rich, creamy filling inside a steamed momo.

Rs. 100/-

Gravy Momo

A unique twist, where the steamed momos are served in a rich, flavorful gravy, often spiced with herbs and tangy sauces. It’s a comforting and hearty dish that combines the soft momo texture with the richness of a saucy accompaniment.

Rs. 120/-

Chicken Momo

A popular choice, featuring minced chicken combined with a blend of fresh herbs, spices, and a touch of garlic and ginger, offering a savory and satisfying bite.

Rs. 150/-

Veg Momo

A classic vegetarian momo, filled with a mix of fresh vegetables like cabbage, carrots, and mushrooms, spiced just right for a flavorful and wholesome experience.

Rs. 80/-

Schezwani Momo

Steamed or fried momos tossed in a fiery Schezwan sauce, offering a bold, tangy, and spicy flavor. A perfect choice for spice lovers looking for a deliciously zesty twist on the classic momo!

Rs. 200/-
